ESONANCE 2015

‘ESONANCE 2015’ is going to be organized by the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering (EEE), the largest department of Islamic University of Technology (IUT). The vision of this event is to bring together students from different institutions and universities across Bangladesh in a delightful environment that will be competitive as well as festive. The participation of students will open up a platform for idea sharing where they can contribute to and benefit from. In this present age, we believe that innovative ideas do not stay confined to any specific group. We expect that students will participate regardless of their disciplines to generate some collaborative, multi-brained contributions.
